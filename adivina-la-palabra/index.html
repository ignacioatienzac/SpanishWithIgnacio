Guess the Word

Welcome to Guess The Word, a Wordle-like game aimed at improving your vocabulary! Pick your level and guess the secret word. You've got 6 tries. After each guess, the tile colors will show you how close you are.

The 6 levels of the Common European Framework (CEFR) are:

  • A1: Beginner Level
  • A2: Elementary Level
  • B1: Intermediate Level
  • B2: Upper-Intermediate Level
  • C1: Advanced Level
  • C2: Mastery Level
